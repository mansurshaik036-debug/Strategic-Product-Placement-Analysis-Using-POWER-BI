# 10 Actionable Business Insights

## Priority 1: End-cap Placement Strategy
**Insight**: End-cap placements generate Rs.17.42M in sales -- the highest of all placement types.
**Action**: Prioritize End-cap placements for strategic SKUs and new product launches.
**Expected Impact**: +6-8% revenue lift for relocated products.
**Owner**: Category Management Team
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 2: Clothing Category Expansion
**Insight**: Clothing leads all categories with Rs.17.31M in sales (34.6% share).
**Action**: Expand shelf depth and inventory allocation for Clothing across all placements.
**Expected Impact**: Rs.2-3M additional revenue.
**Owner**: Merchandising Team
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 3: Electronics Performance Review
**Insight**: Electronics is the lowest-performing category at Rs.16.34M despite competitive pricing.
**Action**: Run A/B tests on price, placement, and promotion before reducing assortment.
**Expected Impact**: Identify Rs.1-2M recovery opportunity or justify SKU rationalization.
**Owner**: Category Management + Pricing Team
**Timeline**: Q3-Q4 2026

## Priority 4: High-Traffic Premium Alignment
**Insight**: High-traffic locations drive Rs.16.84M in sales.
**Action**: Align products with strong positive price spreads to high-footfall zones.
**Expected Impact**: +10-15% margin improvement on relocated SKUs.
**Owner**: Store Operations + Merchandising
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 5: Promotion ROI Evaluation
**Insight**: Promoted products generated Rs.24.55M vs Rs.25.46M for non-promoted -- promotions show negative lift.
**Action**: Evaluate all promotions on estimated margin, not just volume. Cut low-margin promotions.
**Expected Impact**: +2-3% margin improvement.
**Owner**: Marketing + Pricing Team
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 6: Protect Top Performer
**Insight**: Product 115581 is the top performer (Rs.241K sales, Rs.17.7K profit, 7.3% margin).
**Action**: Ensure premium visibility, stock replenishment priority, and End-cap placement.
**Expected Impact**: Protect Rs.241K revenue base.
**Owner**: Inventory Management
**Timeline**: Immediate

## Priority 7: Investigate Zero-Margin Product
**Insight**: Product 176976 has Rs.198K sales but 0.0% estimated margin.
**Action**: Test repricing, negotiate with supplier, or reduce promotion intensity.
**Expected Impact**: Rs.15-20K profit recovery or justify delisting.
**Owner**: Pricing + Procurement Team
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 8: College Student Campaigns
**Insight**: College students drive Rs.12.86M (25.7%) of total sales -- the highest demographic.
**Action**: Design campus-focused campaigns, student discounts, and social media targeting.
**Expected Impact**: Rs.1-2M revenue lift from increased penetration.
**Owner**: Marketing Team
**Timeline**: Q3 2026 (Back-to-school season)

## Priority 9: Outlier Investigation
**Insight**: 2 products flagged as IQR outliers show extreme performance.
**Action**: Investigate whether outliers represent best-practice placement combinations or operational issues.
**Expected Impact**: Identify scalable success patterns or fix operational gaps.
**Owner**: Data Analytics + Operations
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

## Priority 10: Data Infrastructure Improvement
**Insight**: Date, customer, geography, and actual cost fields are absent -- limiting trend and geographic analysis.
**Action**: Add Region, State, City, Store ID, Date, Customer ID, and actual cost fields to future datasets.
**Expected Impact**: Enable full time-series, geographic, and customer-level BI analysis.
**Owner**: Data Engineering + IT
**Timeline**: Q4 2026
